Privacy policy

How Fromentin Industries handles visitor information.

This development-stage website is designed to be static, simple and respectful of visitor privacy. Here is what you should know about data usage.

Data collection

Minimal, transparent and only when visitors choose to contact us.

This website does not collect analytics data, cookies or tracking information. The only visitor information that may be captured is the information you choose to submit through the contact form or by email.

The contact form is static and opens the user’s email client. No form submission data is stored on the server as part of this static website.

Contact messages

Messages are delivered from the user’s email client to the configured recipient address. If you replace the placeholder email, make sure you have a verified business address.

No analytics

The site is built as a static experience without built-in analytics, cookies or third-party tracking tools.

Hosting privacy

Data retained by a hosting provider depends on the chosen platform. This policy covers only the website content and contact flow.

Information security

This site is not designed for sensitive data submission. Do not send confidential or restricted information via the contact form.

How to contact

Use the contact page for inquiries and verification requests.

If you need more information about the initiative, development areas or site usage, use the contact page and verify the email address before publication.
