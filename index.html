01 / About

Built Quietly. Structured Seriously.

Fromentin Industries is a developing Canadian technology and systems concept focused on learning technical design, engineering research, CAD modelling, systems planning, digital systems and future innovation projects.

The long-term mission is to develop practical technology and system-level solutions that strengthen Canadian capability, resilience and future readiness.
