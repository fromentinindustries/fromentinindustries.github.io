About

Fromentin Industries is a developing Canadian technology and systems concept focused on technical design, engineering research, CAD modelling, systems planning, digital systems, and future innovation projects.

The long-term mission is to develop practical technology and system-level solutions that strengthen Canadian capability, resilience, and future readiness.

Services

Technical Design Early-stage design, requirements, constraints, and technical direction. CAD Modelling 3D parts, assemblies, layouts, and prototype visualization. Concept Development Turning raw ideas into structured technical concepts. Systems Planning Planning how hardware, software, people, and processes connect. Technology Research Researching emerging tools, systems, and future capability needs. Engineering Documentation Reports, diagrams, briefs, proposals, and technical project records. Digital Systems Support Servers, automation, information systems, and technical workflows.

Status

Fromentin Industries is currently in private development.

Current work is focused on building the brand foundation, documenting projects, developing technical skills, and organizing future service areas.
