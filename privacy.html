Website Access, Privacy, and Development-Stage Notice

This page exists because visitors are required to acknowledge the status and terms of the Fromentin Industries website before using the public site.

Development-stage status

Fromentin Industries is a private development-stage Canadian technology and systems brand. The public pages are written professionally and are prepared as launch-ready public material, but the company, service structure, backend systems, project records, and operating documents are still under development.

No statement on this website should be read as a claim that Fromentin Industries is currently operating at the scale of an established defence, aerospace, industrial, engineering, or government contractor.

Permitted use

This website may be viewed for general information, portfolio review, contact, and brand-development purposes. Visitors may not copy, mirror, scrape, impersonate, commercially reuse, redistribute, or misleadingly reference Fromentin Industries material without written permission.

Intellectual property

The website structure, wording, layout, logos, slogans, visual identity, documentation concepts, service-area wording, project organization, and development-area language are part of the Fromentin Industries brand system unless otherwise stated.

Privacy

The site is static and does not currently operate a user account system, database, analytics dashboard, or backend login. The contact form is routed through FormSubmit to the Fromentin Industries contact inbox. Information submitted through the form may include name, email, request type, and message content.

The terms gate stores only a local browser acknowledgement using localStorage. This is stored on the visitor’s own device and is used so the notice does not appear on every visit after acceptance.

Future backend notice

The website contains dormant placeholders for a future login system, dashboard, project registry, document vault, administrative panel, and API structure. These placeholders are non-functional in the current static GitHub Pages version and do not collect login data.

No sensitive operational claims

References to defence-adjacent, security, aerospace, infrastructure, or industrial systems are public, lawful, non-classified, and support-oriented. They describe long-term development areas and research direction, not classified work, procurement status, military endorsement, or active government contract performance.

Contact

Questions about this website, its wording, or use of Fromentin Industries material may be sent to contact.fromentinindustries@gmail.com.
