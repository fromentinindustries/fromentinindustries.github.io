Privacy policy

Minimal by design.

This static website is designed to present information without built-in analytics, advertising pixels or server-side contact-form storage.

The public site does not include built-in analytics, cookies or third-party tracking tools. Information is only provided when a visitor chooses to contact Fromentin Industries by email.

The contact form opens the visitor’s email client using a mailto link. The message is sent by the visitor’s own email provider to the configured recipient address.

Basic technical logs may be handled by the hosting provider. This policy covers the website content and public contact flow, not separate provider infrastructure.
