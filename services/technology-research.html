Technology Research

Understanding what matters before building what is next.

Technology Research focuses on learning, comparing, and documenting emerging tools, systems, trends, and opportunities connected to future Canadian capability.

Back to Services Contact
Overview

Purpose

Technology Research is about building informed direction. Before a project is designed or built, it helps to understand existing solutions, emerging technology, technical limits, and real-world needs.

The purpose is to avoid shallow ideas and build stronger concepts based on research, comparison, and practical analysis.

Focus

What This Includes

Technology Scanning

Researching tools, systems, engineering methods, and innovation areas that could support future projects.

Comparison

Comparing solutions, advantages, disadvantages, costs, limitations, and possible improvements.

Capability Gaps

Identifying areas where Canada could benefit from stronger domestic technology, infrastructure, or systems.

Applications

Possible Project Uses

Technology Research can support aerospace studies, defence-adjacent concepts, infrastructure ideas, automation projects, AI-assisted systems, energy systems, digital tools, and engineering portfolios.
