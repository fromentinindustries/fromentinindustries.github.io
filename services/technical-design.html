Technical Design

Early-stage engineering concepts, practical systems, and structured design work.

Overview

Technical Design focuses on turning ideas into structured, usable concepts. This includes defining the purpose, requirements, constraints, layout, function, and future development path of a project.

Focus Areas

  • Mechanical and technical concept design
  • System layouts and early design planning
  • Problem definition and requirements planning
  • Design improvement and practical solution development
  • Project structure, constraints, and technical direction

Status
