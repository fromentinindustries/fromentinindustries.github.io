# Bill's Moving Sales Brief

## Why Bill's Moving needs a website

Bill's Moving appears in Brockville listings, but it lacks a dedicated website that clearly presents the phone number, address, services, service area and quote process.

A polished website gives local customers confidence, improves visibility for Brockville moving searches and makes it easier to convert directory interest into calls.

## Problems with directory-only presence

- Directory listings are often sparse and inconsistent.
- Customers may not trust businesses without a real website.
- There is no owned page for service details, local area coverage or direct contact.
- Phone calls are harder to convert without clear pricing, process and location context.

## What this website fixes

- Presents Bill's Moving as a trustworthy Brockville mover.
- Highlights local service area and moving support clearly.
- Places the phone number and quote request near the top of the page.
- Provides a clean mobile-first experience for urgent moving searchers.
- Gives the owner a ready-to-launch static website concept.

## How it helps generate more calls

- Big phone CTA and sticky contact bar for mobile users.
- Local service area copy targeted to Brockville and nearby communities.
- Quote section encourages customers to share move details.
- FAQ builds trust and reduces friction before calling.

## Suggested pitch message

"Hi, I noticed Bill's Moving is listed online in Brockville directories, but I could not find a proper dedicated website. I built a clean mobile-ready demo that gives customers your services, service area, phone number, quote form and local search structure. It is ready for owner verification and could be launched quickly."

## Suggested price range

- Starter launch website: CAD $350–$650
- Includes static website, mobile optimization, local SEO structure and deployment support.
- Optional add-on: CAD $35–$75/month for updates, uptime checks and small edits.

## Questions to ask the owner before launch

- Is the phone number and address correct?
- What email should the quote form send to?
- Do you want to accept website quote requests or calls only?
- What specific moving services should be listed?
- Do you want to add operating hours?
- Would you like a custom domain or business email?

## Owner verification needed

This demo uses public listing information. Confirm the business name, phone number, email address, service details and address before going live.
